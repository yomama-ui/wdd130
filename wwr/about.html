White Water Rafting Co.

Our mission is to bring unforgettable rafting adventures to thrill-seekers and nature lovers. Whether you're a first-timer or an experienced rafter, we have something for everyone!

History

Founded in 2005, White Water Rafting Co. began with a single raft and a dream to conquer the wild rivers of the west. Since then, we’ve guided thousands safely down raging waters and created memories that last a lifetime. We value safety, adventure, and unforgettable moments in the outdoors.
